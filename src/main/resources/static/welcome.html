KLRAMS Console

The operational console for Kerala's road network — every metre surveyed, measured and on the map. Road inventory, pavement condition, traffic and geotechnical data brought together in one GIS platform for data-driven maintenance planning. Operated by the RMMS Cell, KHRI.

What it does

Core modules of the platform.

Field data collection across Kerala's road network — surveyed, sampled and tested by the RMMS Cell.

Road Asset Inventory

Network-level inventory of roads — classification, ownership and measured length.

Pavement Condition & PCI

Condition surveys and Pavement Condition Index (IRC:82-2023) for maintenance prioritisation.

Traffic Surveys

Traffic volume, ADT, peak-hour and direction-wise counts at survey stations.

FWD / Geotechnical

Falling Weight Deflectometer deflection (D0) and pavement structural condition.

GIS Network Viewer

Interactive map of the road network with survey video and condition layers.

Government Orders

Repository of Government Orders and circulars relevant to the road network.

About
